Adoption Terms and Conditions
Adoption Agreement

By adopting a pet through our organization, you agree to provide a loving, safe, and permanent home for the animal.

Care Requirements

You must provide proper food, water, shelter, veterinary care, and companionship for your adopted pet.

Home Check

We reserve the right to conduct home visits before and after adoption to ensure the pet's welfare.

Return Policy

If you can no longer care for your adopted pet, you must return them to our organization rather than rehoming them yourself.

Legal Ownership

Ownership of the pet will be transferred only after the adoption fee is paid in full and all paperwork is completed.
